    About

    At the UCSI Preschool Centre, Bandar Springhill, we believe each child should receive quality care and a developmentally appropriate education. Instead of restrictive rote learning, the Centre focuses on play as the foundation for future academic learning. Active thinking, investigating, and experimenting as well as adult-guided instruction provide children opportunities to learn and function at their own developmental age level. On a daily basis, our students engage in exploratory learning that blends both the teacher-directed approach and the child-directed approach.

    We aim to not only deliver a balanced academic experience, but to also create a learning environment that shapes children into well-rounded global citizens. We ensure that character development themes – such as cultural diversity, acceptance and cooperation, to name a few – are woven into our daily lessons and are ever present. And in so doing, not only are we able to create a cohesive classroom environment, but we are also able to promote healthy social and emotional development in children.

    Our Programmes

    Every day, our students will conduct exploratory study and implement the “teacher guides, student-centered” teaching principles. We firmly believe that a child’s educational development should be comprehensive, and we implement teaching in learn from play. To this end, our mission is not just to provide a balanced academic experience, but also create an environment for children to learn.

    The UCSI Preschool Centre Nursery Programme for children aged between 1.5 years old to 2.5 years old is based on the understanding that the early formative years are the most critical in a child’s development.

    The indoor classrooms and outdoor spaces are set up to promote active learning and encourage different types of play.

    A stimulating environment which offers a range of safe equipment and activities is provided that allows children to explore, discover, stimulate and experiment through sensory play. Our Nursery programme is conducted from 8.30am to 12.30pm from Monday to Friday.

    Fully-trained staff experienced in early years teaching provides structured and fun activities for the children. When the children are ready to step forward into the next stage in their development they join Preschool 1.

    Preschool 1

    Preschool 1 is suitable for children aged 3. At this stage in a child’s life, independent behavior has started. Self-help skills are encouraged and supported.

    Children will learn about routines which will foster language skills and the ability to concentrate will be extended. Through a diverse range of structured and unstructured learning activities, our teachers promote learning that is purposeful and enjoyable for children.

    Our three-year-old programme focuses on the primary areas of development. These are language, arts, literacy, mathematics, science, social and emotional development, gross and fine motor development, as well as character building. These are all part of the Life Essentials your child will learn and build upon at the UCSI Preschool Centre, Bandar Springhill.

    Preschool 3

    Preschool 2 is suitable for children aged 4. It is a fun, play-based environment that encourages learning through experimenting, trial and error, watching, listening and participating. As preschoolers gain more self-esteem, they feel ready to take on the world.

    Our Preschool 2 curriculum enhances that confidence by providing activities to help 4-year old children become problem solvers and develop a love of lifelong learning.

    Through independent exploration, structured activities, and hands-on learning, children develop a variety of skills and knowledge in areas like early literacy, mathematics, science, mandarin and social skills.

    UCSI Preschool Centre offers a safe secure environment for your child. In our programme each child is appreciated and accepted at the level of their maturity and ability. The child is given the opportunity to learn to grow and develop in a climate of genuine affection and warmth.

    Preschool 3

    Preschool 3 is suitable for children aged 5. A predictable yet flexible daily routine responds to and encourages children’s natural curiosity and desire to learn. It provides consistency and helps them gain a sense of security and confidence to participate in the different parts of the programme.

    UCSI Preschool Centre provides stimulating, challenging and accessible materials that are inviting and aesthetically pleasing to a child. This specially created environment entices the child to explore, discover, manipulate and learn.

    The resources and materials offered represent a wide curriculum, including literacy, numeracy, science, computer studies, music, art & craft, gardening, and physical activities.

    Children are encouraged to develop their planning, implementing, decision-making, problem solving and reflecting skills through the plan-do-review cycle of the daily programme. They also have the opportunity for individual, small and large group interactions through adult initiated activities.
